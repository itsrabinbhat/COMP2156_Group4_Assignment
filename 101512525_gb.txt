🍏 George Brown College 

📓 About ~
George Brown College is a College of Applied arts and Technology located in Downtown Toronto.
Knowed for its hands-on learning approach

They offer a range of programs in fields such as:
- Technology ⚙️
- Health sciences 🫀
- Business 📊
- Arts 🎨  

Programs offered 🧑‍🏫
- Computer Systems Technology (T147)
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc.N.) (S118)
- Business Administration - Accounting (B107)
- Concept Art for Entertainment (G407)

Community 🧩
The College is commited to supporting diverse and inclusive learning environments and puts focus on preparing students for successful careers in their respective feilds.
