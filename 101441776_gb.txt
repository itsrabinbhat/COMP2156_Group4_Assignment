George Brown College is a public college that emphasizes hands-on learning, industry partnerships and real-world experience.
They offer career-focused programs in business, technology, healthcare, design and hospitality.
One of the technology programs George Brown College offers is Computer Systems Technology. 
One of the business programs George Brown College offers is Business Administration for Accounting.
One of the healthcare programs George Brown College offers is Dental Technician.
One of the design programs George Brown College offers is Graphic Design.
One of the hospitality programs George Brown College offers is Tourism and Hospitality Management.
