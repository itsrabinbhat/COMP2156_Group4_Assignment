COMP2156 DevOps
In this course students will learn in-depth of DevOps principles and its best practices that drive efficiency and collaboration
in modern development environments. Students will also learn through hands-on experience using tools such as Ansible and Git
and gain an understanding of essential data formats including XML, JSON, YAML and YANG.
Students will also implement DevOps practices such as continous integration (CI), automation and collaboration techniques to streamline the software development lifecycle.
